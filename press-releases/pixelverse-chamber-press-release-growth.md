## PixelVerse Studios Joins North New Jersey Chamber of Commerce to Bring Growth-Focused Web Design and SEO to Local Businesses

**Bergen County, NJ, May 20, 2026** — PixelVerse Studios has joined the North New Jersey Chamber of Commerce to help businesses across Bergen County and the tri-state area turn their websites into stronger tools for visibility, lead generation, and growth.

PixelVerse Studios builds custom websites, SEO strategies, website audits, lead intake systems, client dashboards, and digital workflows for businesses that need more than a template site. The studio focuses on helping companies improve search visibility, convert visitors into inquiries, and manage their online presence with greater clarity.

"Businesses need websites that work as hard as they do," said the PixelVerse Studios team. "Our goal is to help chamber members strengthen their digital foundation, attract better opportunities, and build systems that support long-term growth."

As a chamber member offer, PixelVerse Studios is providing **25% off setup costs** and an additional **10% off monthly costs** for North New Jersey Chamber members who sign on before **August 1, 2026**.

PixelVerse Studios serves Bergen County, the tri-state area, and businesses anywhere digitally.

## About PixelVerse Studios
