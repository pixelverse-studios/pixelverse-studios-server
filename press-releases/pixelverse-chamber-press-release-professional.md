## PixelVerse Studios Joins North New Jersey Chamber of Commerce to Support Local Businesses with Web Design, SEO, and Digital Growth

**Bergen County, NJ, May 20, 2026** — PixelVerse Studios has joined the North New Jersey Chamber of Commerce, expanding its connection to the Bergen County business community and the broader tri-state area.

PixelVerse Studios provides custom website development, local SEO, website audits, lead-generation systems, contact forms, client dashboards, and digital strategy for businesses that want a stronger online presence. Through its chamber membership, the studio aims to help local companies improve how they are found, evaluated, and contacted online.

"Joining the North New Jersey Chamber of Commerce gives the PixelVerse Studios team a stronger way to connect with local business owners and support the digital growth of the region," said the PixelVerse Studios team. "A company's website should be more than a digital brochure. It should help build trust, improve visibility, and create new opportunities."

As part of its chamber introduction, PixelVerse Studios is offering North New Jersey Chamber members **25% off website setup costs** and an additional **10% off monthly costs** for businesses that sign on before **August 1, 2026**.

PixelVerse Studios works with businesses throughout Bergen County, the tri-state area, and clients anywhere digitally.
