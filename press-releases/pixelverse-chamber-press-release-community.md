## PixelVerse Studios Joins North New Jersey Chamber of Commerce to Help Local Businesses Grow Online

**Bergen County, NJ, May 20, 2026** — PixelVerse Studios has joined the North New Jersey Chamber of Commerce, marking a new step in the company's commitment to supporting local businesses across Bergen County and the tri-state area.

The PixelVerse Studios team helps businesses create websites that are modern, easy to use, and built to generate real results. Its services include custom web design, website development, local SEO, website audits, contact forms, lead intake systems, and digital strategy.

"Local businesses are the foundation of strong communities, and we want to help them compete confidently online," said the PixelVerse Studios team. "By joining the North New Jersey Chamber of Commerce, we're excited to build relationships with business owners who care deeply about their work and their communities."

To welcome fellow chamber members, PixelVerse Studios is offering **25% off setup costs** and an additional **10% off monthly costs** for businesses that sign on before **August 1, 2026**.

PixelVerse Studios serves businesses in Bergen County, throughout the tri-state area, and anywhere digitally.
