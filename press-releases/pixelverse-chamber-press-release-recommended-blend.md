## PixelVerse Studios Joins North New Jersey Chamber of Commerce to Help Local Businesses Grow Online

**Custom web design, local SEO, website audits, and digital growth systems now available to chamber members with an introductory member offer.**

**Bergen County, NJ, May 20, 2026** — PixelVerse Studios has joined the North New Jersey Chamber of Commerce, strengthening its connection to the Bergen County business community and expanding its support for companies across the tri-state area.

PixelVerse Studios helps businesses build stronger online presences through custom website development, local SEO, website audits, lead-generation systems, contact forms, client dashboards, and ongoing digital strategy. The studio works with businesses that want their websites to do more than look professional; they want them to improve visibility, build trust, and create measurable opportunities.

"Joining the North New Jersey Chamber of Commerce gives the PixelVerse Studios team a meaningful way to connect with local business owners and support the growth of the region," said the PixelVerse Studios team. "We believe a strong website should be a practical business tool, helping companies get found, communicate clearly, and turn interest into action."

As part of its chamber membership, PixelVerse Studios is offering North New Jersey Chamber members **25% off setup costs** and an additional **10% off monthly costs** for businesses that sign on before **August 1, 2026**.

While based in Bergen County, NJ, PixelVerse Studios serves businesses throughout the tri-state area and works with clients anywhere digitally.
